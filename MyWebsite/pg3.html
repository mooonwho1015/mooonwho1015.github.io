The Big Dragon

A great shadow rises ancient and proud. You bow your head in respect, and the dragon lets you pass unharmed. You'll tell this story for years.

About the Dragon

  • The dragon guards the hidden treasure deep within the mountain.
  • Its scales shimmer like gold under the sunlight.
  • Legends say the dragon protects the land from evil spirits.
  • You realize courage, not weapons, will decide your fate.

Your Encounter

  1. You step closer, feeling the ground tremble beneath your feet.
  2. The dragon opens its eyes and studies you carefully.
  3. You speak calmly, showing respect instead of fear.
  4. The dragon nods slowly and allows you to pass unharmed.
  5. You leave the mountain, your heart filled with courage and awe.
