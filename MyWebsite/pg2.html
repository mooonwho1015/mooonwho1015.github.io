Green Forest

The Trees seem to lean together, whispering your name. A soft path divides one bends towards a faint river song. The other climbs towards an old mountain trail.

At the Forest Edge

  • The forest air feels cool and fresh, carrying the scent of pine and damp earth.
  • Every step lands on soft moss, muffling your sound and calming your heartbeat.
  • Birdsong echoes between tall trunks, blending with the distant rush of a stream.
  • Gentle beams of sunlight scatter through the canopy like tiny lanterns of hope.
  • In this green silence, you feel both small and infinite, part of something timeless.

How would you feel

  1. You take a deep breath, feeling the crisp forest air fill your lungs.
  2. Sunlight filters through the canopy, dancing on your hands like gold.
  3. You hear the distant call of a bird, soft and comforting.
  4. The gentle rustle of leaves makes you feel safe and alive.
  5. You close your eyes for a moment, letting the forest quiet your thoughts.
